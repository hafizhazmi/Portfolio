My Journey into Tech!

My journey started with numbers. Armed with a mathematics degree, I dived into the world of finance, analyzing economic trends and making sense of complex data. But curiosity led me further. I wanted to create, not just calculate.

That’s when I discovered the Power Platform. Low-code and no-code development opened my eyes to a new way of solving problems, automating workflows, and building solutions with speed. It was the perfect bridge between logic and creativity.

But I wasn’t stopping there. The world of frontend development caught my attention, and I challenged myself to build my portfolio website from scratch using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in record time. The thrill of crafting seamless user experiences and bringing ideas to life through code has fueled my passion ever since.

Now, I’m on a mission to blend problem-solving, technology, and design to create impactful digital experiences. This is just the beginning of my journey, and I’d love to connect with like-minded learners and builders. Let’s collaborate, share knowledge, and build something great together!
